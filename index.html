PIG DICE GAME

🎲 Game Rules

Welcome to the Dice Duel!
Battle it out against the computer — roll, hold, and race to 100 points.

  1. 🧍 Player 1 starts first: Click Roll Dice to roll. Each roll adds points to your current round score.
  2. ⚠️ Beware of rolling a 1: If you roll a 1, you lose your current round’s points and your turn ends immediately.
  3. ✋ Play it safe with Hold: Click Hold to bank your round points and pass the turn to the computer.
  4. 🤖 The Computer plays automatically: It rolls a few times unless it rolls a 1 — then it loses its round score!
  5. 🏆 First to reach 100 points wins: Luck and timing are everything. Be bold, but don’t get greedy!

💡 Tip: Take smart risks early, but know when to hold your ground!
